Our team specializes in the installation of custom-made awnings. We value our clients' time and money, therefore we work quickly, efficiently and inexpensively. The Tucha-T company is engaged in the production and sale of awning coverings of various types. In our work we use high quality and durable PVC material from Germany, Belgium, Spain and Korea (SEDO, Polytex, Sioline and Polymar). We will provide you with probes for making the coating.

About The Company

Our company has a decade of experience in making various pvc fabric products. The use of modern equipment and branded fabrics from leading foreign countries allows us to manufacture high quality products that can be used for more than 5-7 years. Awning coverings for cars are made by heating the material with an electric hairdryer, which helps the fabric at the joints to become homogeneous and durable, and also significantly increases the working life. Thus, we prepare sealed and high-quality awnings for GAZelle, TATA, awning covers for trucks and other trucks.
